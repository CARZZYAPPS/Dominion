Resource Economy Credits (💰) are for supplies and Mobilization; Alloy (🔩) is for Lab Research and Fortress Defense; Food (🥫) is your energy where 1 battle consumes 1 unit.

Combat Strength Your total Strength (⚡) is calculated by combining your Army multiplied by Skill, your Mercenaries multiplied by Generals, and your Defense multiplied by two.

Scouting Tiers Scout (Green) is capped at 70% power for safe training; Tactical (Blue) is capped at 110% for fair fights; Overlord (Red) is capped at 150% for high-risk loot.

World Eater Boss A unique boss spawns every 5 levels, hard-locked at 150% strength relative to your rank, providing massive XP and resource rewards upon victory.

Mobilization Protocol This Lab upgrade doubles your current Army size; the first 2 uses are a flat 500 Credits and 250 Alloy, but 3+ uses scale to 5 * Army² in Credits.

Mercenary Generals Buying Generals in the Lab provides a permanent, stackable multiplier to your Mercenary combat power pool, making contractors more effective.

Infinite Training The Training upgrade increases Army Skill indefinitely and triggers visual "MOVE IT!" shouts accompanied by commander audio effects.

Neural Link Automation Unlocked at Level 75, this system auto-scouts and fights the largest beatable target every 3 seconds until a defeat is detected.

Power Surge Overload By consuming 50 Food, your power is doubled for 30 seconds and your Strength meter hits 200%, followed by a persistent 60-second cooldown.

Loyalty Commissions A milestone system that automatically grants a free commission of +15 Mercenaries every 15 levels regardless of your play style.

Immersive Feedback The system utilizes screen shaking, metallic clashing sparks, synthesized commander screams, and red alert background strobes during surges.

Persistent Data All progress, including cheat snapshots and active cooldown timers, is saved to your browser's local storage and cannot be bypassed by refreshing.
